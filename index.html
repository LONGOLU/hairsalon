Blush & Curls

Mannie Bajwa

My name is Mannie Bajwa. I am an esthetician and a hair stylist. I earned my certificate in cosmetology from delmar College Calgary in 2010. I have completed numbers of advanced esthetic courses by ABS and esthetic institute of calgary.

I was born and raised in India, Calgary been home since 2007. Growing up India, I always have this passion for styling hair watching bridal make up. My cousin who took beauty school back in India I love watching her doing threading on others. I have self taught myself theading and its become my specialty. I found threading is very neat and unique technique of shaping eye brows.

My clients are the favorite part of my work. My inspiration comes from my clients, who are constantly pushing me to be the best version of myself and growing professionally and personally.
